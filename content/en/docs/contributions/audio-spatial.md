## trajectoires

**Fork** · [github.com/dewiweb/trajectoires](https://github.com/dewiweb/trajectoires)

Mobile/web application for spatial audio control via OSC. Fork of the original, adapted and used as a working base.

- Language: Objective-C
- Usage: OSC control of sound sources from mobile

## SPARTA

**Fork** · [github.com/dewiweb/SPARTA](https://github.com/dewiweb/SPARTA)

Collection of VST audio plugins for spatial audio production, reproduction and visualisation. Suite developed by Aalto University.

- Language: C++
- Licence: GPL-3.0
- [Original project](http://research.spa.aalto.fi/projects/sparta_vsts/)
